You Can Learn 4 Languages Here

ENGLISH

English is still the most-spoken language around the world with 1,132 million speakers. not due to its native speakers alone, but because many others learn it as a second language. It is well worth the effort as our lingua franca and a crucial tool for business between cultures!

SPANISH (Español)

How many people speak Spanish? it is 534 million speakers. Learn this language if…you want to learn a language that is spoken as it is written but still love a good grammar challenge.

JAPANESE (日本語)

With over 122 million native speakers, Japanese is one of the world’s most commonly spoken languages. The vast majority of Japanese speakers live in Japan. Japanese has a unique writing system. In fact, most Japanese text uses a mixture of kanji, characters that come from Chinese, and kana, characters that correspond to sounds.

MANDARIN CHINESE (现代汉语)

Mandarin Chinese have 1,117 million speakers. Mandarin Chinese has the largest number of native speakers of any language and is spoken by 70% of all Chinese speakers. Many fear learning Mandarin due to its 50,000 different characters. We think it’s worth the dedication!
